Our Story

A Home by the
Punnamada Lake

Nestled on the banks of the famous Punnamada Lake — home to the legendary Nehru Trophy Boat Race — our family homestay has been welcoming travellers from around the world for over a decade.

We believe in giving you the real Kerala experience: waking up to the sounds of birds and rippling water, savouring authentic home-cooked breakfast, watching the sunrise paint the backwaters gold, and ending your evening watching the stars reflect on the lake.

Our warm family will make you feel not like a guest, but like you've come home.

Experiences

More Than a Stay —
A Kerala Journey

We arrange everything so you can simply be present and soak in the magic of Alappuzha.

🚤

Shikara Boat Rides

Drift through the serene backwater canals at sunrise or sunset on a traditional shikara. Watch local life unfold on the water.

🏆

Nehru Trophy Boat Race

Witness the iconic Nehru Trophy Boat Race on Punnamada Lake — one of India's most thrilling sporting spectacles, right at your doorstep.

🐟

Local Fishing

Join our host family for an early morning fishing trip on the lake using traditional Kerala nets and methods.

🌿

Ayurveda Wellness

We arrange authentic Ayurvedic massage and wellness sessions with certified practitioners to rejuvenate body and mind.

🌾

Village Walk

Explore the backwater villages, paddy fields, coconut groves, and local life on a guided morning walk with our family.

🍳

Kerala Cooking Class

Learn to cook authentic Kerala dishes — fish curry, appam, karimeen pollichathu — with our hosts in a hands-on kitchen session.
